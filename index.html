HELLO

NexRez
Independent hotel reservation revenue and online marketing has never been as easy as it is with The NexRez Central Reservation System (CRS)

“THE cloud based system for independent hotels”.

￼We are proud to offer your independent hotel the same world class technology that major hotel brands use to connect seamlessly to Expedia, Booking.com, Orbitz, Travelocity, Priceline, Hotels.com the GDS frame work, and 200 plus travel websites.

Hotel independence has never been this easy!

SERVICES

  • Online Travel Sites

    Reservations with Expedia, Booking.com, Orbitz, Priceline, Hotels.com, Hotwire, Google, Kayak, Trivago, Tripadvisor, & 200 plus online travel sites. Mobile booking engine platform – mobile connected

  • Global Distribution Systems

    Reservations from 600,000 plus worldwide travel agents with Sabre, Worldspan, Amadeus, Apollo, Pegasus, and Galileo GDS platform. We are Travel Agent connected

  • Cloud Server

    Stays connected with a cloud based server. Access your properties information anywhere in the world, from any type of device with internet including iPhone, Android, Mac, PC, or Tab. Change rates, availability, inventory, and content on the go

  • PMS Interfaces Available

    CRS / PMS interface eliminates manual data entry and optimizes revenue opportunities. An interface can significantly increase occupancy and ADR through a joint effort to sell all your room availability, at the right price.

  • Credit Card Processing

    Because NexRez actually owns a credit card processing company, anyone who uses our system gets deep discounts on all their credit card processing that even the “Big Chains” can’t get. This can be a much as a 20% to 30% savings on your credit card processing fees. We don’t have a deal with a processor, we are the processor.

  • Analysis

    Real time reporting and analysis on market trends including price point strategy, display strategies, online travel site promotion efficiency, distribution mix and performance trends. Make well informed decisions for your hotel by using REAL data.

  • Support

    24/7 client support from experienced GDS experts and hoteliers just like you. We are dedicated to your success and we will support your business goals. We are the only company that gives you direct access to our owners. We are on the hook to deliver results and an outstanding client experience, give our references a call.

  • Results

    Our success depends on YOUR results. We are a performance based company that believes “there is nothing more expensive than an unsold room”. Fees are tied directly to performance and the reservations produced.
